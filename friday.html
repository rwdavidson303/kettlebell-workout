Power Day

Friday Workout

Goal: Maximum force per rep — heavier bells, explosive intent
Pace: Deliberate — HR to 130-135 bpm (~45-60 sec rest)
Time: 60-70 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Bell Notes Demo
1 Kettlebell Swings 6 x 10 HEAVIEST Maximum hip snap. Squeeze glutes HARD at top. Every rep like it's the only one. Watch Demo
2 High Pulls 5 x 10 Heavy Aggressive pull. Elbows should fly up. Power from the hips. Watch Demo
3 Side (Golf) Swings 4 x 10 Heavy Explosive rotation. Feel the core and obliques drive the movement. Watch Demo
4 Waist Halos 2 x 12 Light-Med Active recovery between ballistics and compounds. Alternate direction each set. Watch Demo
5 Goblet Squats 4 x 10 HEAVIEST Hold KB at chest, full depth squat. Heaviest bell of the week. Drive through heels. Watch Demo
6 Rows 4 x 10/arm HEAVIEST Slow, heavy pulls. 2-second hold at the top. Feel the lats engage fully. Watch Demo
7 Push Outs 4 x 10 Heavy Push with intent. Full lockout. Controlled 2-second return. Form Cues
8 Head Halos 2 x 12 Light-Med Active recovery. Heavier halo for more trap and shoulder demand. Form Cues
9 Overhead Press 4 x 8/arm Heavy Strongest pressing effort of the week. Lock out fully. 8 per arm per set. Watch Demo
10 Bicep Curls + Press 3 x 10 Med-Heavy Step up the weight. Full curl, full press, full lockout. Form Cues
11 Tricep Extensions 3 x 10 Medium Heavier than Wednesday. Slow down (2 sec), explosive press up. Form Cues
12 Side Extensions 3 x 10/side Heavy Heavier bell. Slow controlled lean. Feel every inch of the stretch. Watch Demo
13 Farmer's Walk 3 x 40s HEAVIEST Heaviest bells available. Walk with purpose, shoulders packed. 40 seconds per walk. Watch Demo
Total: ~530 reps — Heavier loads = greater strength and muscle-toning stimulus

Cool-Down (5 Minutes)

  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes
  • Full-body stretch: hip flexors, hamstrings, chest, lats — 30 sec each
  • Shoulder stretch: 30 sec per side
