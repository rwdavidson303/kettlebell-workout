Metabolic Day

Metabolic Workout

Goal: Maximum EPOC — burn calories for up to 38 hours post-workout
Rest: 30-45 sec between circuits
Tempo: 2-0-1 (controlled, fluid)
Time: ~45 minutes

Compound primer first, then two circuits of 3 rounds each, finishing with push-ups and farmer's walk.

Block 1 — Compound Primer (Straight Sets, 45s Rest)
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes Demo
1 DB Thruster 3 x 12 20-25 lb 45s Squat to press in one fluid motion. Form Cues
2 DB Romanian Deadlift 3 x 12 20-25 lb 45s Controlled hinge. Keep tension throughout. Form Cues
Block 2 — Circuit A (3 Rounds, 30s Rest Between Rounds)
# Exercise Reps Weight Notes Demo
3a Reverse Lunge 10/leg 15-20 lb Alternating legs, steady tempo. Form Cues
3b Incline DB Press 12 15-20 lb Lighter, keep rhythm. Form Cues
3c One-Arm Row 10/arm 15-20 lb No bench needed, hinge forward. Form Cues
3d DB Hip Thrust 12 20-25 lb Shoulders on bench. Quick reps, full extension. Form Cues
Block 3 — Circuit B (3 Rounds, 30s Rest Between Rounds)
# Exercise Reps Weight Notes Demo
4a Goblet Squat 15 20-25 lb One DB at chest. Full depth, keep moving. Form Cues
4b Chest-Supported Row 10 15-20 lb Face-down on incline bench. Row both DBs. Form Cues
4c OH Tricep Extension 12 10-15 lb One DB, both hands. Full ROM. Form Cues
4d Lateral Raise 12 10-15 lb Controlled. Don't let form break. Form Cues
Block 4 — Finisher
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes
5 Push-Ups 2 x max Bodyweight 30s Full ROM, chest to floor. Elevate feet if 20+.
6 Farmer's Walk (45 sec) 3 x 1 25-30 lb 30s Heaviest DBs. Walk with purpose. 45 seconds.
Total: ~400 reps — 27 working sets — 2 circuits of 3 rounds + finisher
