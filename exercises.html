Exercise Library

Illustrated form guides and cues for all 13 exercises

1

Kettlebell Swings

Power
Watch: Cavemantraining Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hike the bell back between your legs like a football snap
  • Snap hips forward explosively — arms are ropes, power comes from the hip
  • Lock out at top: squeeze glutes, brace abs, body is a straight line
  • Bell reaches chest height, NOT overhead
  • Sharp exhale at top (through pursed lips), inhale on the way down
  • Keep your back flat — never round the lower back
Monday: 5×15 (Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 4×20 (Med-Heavy)  |  Friday: 6×10 (Heaviest)
2

High Pulls

Power
Full Tutorial: KettlebellsWorkouts.com

Form Cues

  • Starts like a one-arm swing
  • As bell rises, pull elbow back and up — elbow should point to the ceiling
  • Bell comes to chin/chest height, NOT overhead
  • Hip-driven, NOT an arm pull — the hips generate the force
  • Keep the bell close to your body on the pull
  • Snap the hips on every rep — don't use the arm to muscle the bell up
Monday: 4×12 (Med-Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 4×15 (Med)  |  Friday: 5×10 (Heavy)
3

Side (Golf) Swings

Power
Watch: Cavemantraining Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand upright with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Swing the bell in a rotational arc across your body
  • Power comes from the core and hip rotation, not the arms
  • Control the rotation — don't let momentum whip you around
  • Keep feet planted and knees slightly bent
  • Alternate directions: 10 each way per set (or full sets per direction)
Monday: 3×15 (Med)  |  Wednesday: 3×20 (Med)  |  Friday: 4×10 (Heavy)
4

Waist Halos (Around the Body)

Mobility
Watch: Onnit Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Pass the bell around your waist in a circular motion
  • Transfer the bell from hand to hand behind your back
  • Keep your core engaged and torso still
  • Alternate direction each set
  • Smooth, continuous motion — don't pause at the hand-off
  • Keep your eyes forward and posture tall
Monday: 2×15 (Light)  |  Wednesday: 2×20 (Light)  |  Friday: 2×12 (Light-Med)
5

Goblet Squats

Compound Lower
Watch: Kettlebell Goblet Squat Demo

Form Cues

  • Hold the KB at chest height with both hands on the horns
  • Squat to full depth — hip crease below knee
  • Knees track over toes — push them out, don't let them cave in
  • Drive through your heels to stand back up
  • Keep your chest up and back flat throughout
  • Pause 1 second at the bottom before driving up
Monday: 4×12 (Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 3×15 (Med-Heavy)  |  Friday: 4×10 (Heaviest)
6

Bent-Over Rows

Compound Pull
Watch: Cavemantraining Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hinge at hips ~45 degrees, flat back, core braced
  • Pull elbow past the ribcage, not just to the body
  • Squeeze the shoulder blade at the top for 1 second
  • Control the lowering — don't let gravity do the work
  • Keep your non-working hand on your knee or a bench for support
  • Don't rotate your torso — keep shoulders square
Monday: 4×12/arm (Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 3×15/arm (Med-Heavy)  |  Friday: 4×10/arm (Heaviest)
7

Push Outs

Compound Push
Full Tutorial: Strongandfit.com

Form Cues

  • Hold the kettlebell by the horns at chest height
  • Press the bell horizontally forward until arms are fully extended
  • Keep your core braced — don't arch the lower back
  • Squeeze your chest at full extension
  • Return slowly to the chest — controlled 2-second return
  • Shoulders stay down and back throughout the movement
Monday: 4×12 (Med-Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 3×15 (Med)  |  Friday: 4×10 (Heavy)
8

Head Halos

Mobility
Kettlebell Kings Tutorial Live Lean TV Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hold the bell by the horns (upside down) at chest height
  • Circle the bell around your head in a smooth arc
  • Keep the bell close to your head — don't let it drift out
  • Alternate direction each set
  • Keep your core braced and torso completely still
  • Slow and controlled — this is mobility work, not a race
Monday: 2×15 (Light)  |  Wednesday: 2×20 (Light)  |  Friday: 2×12 (Light-Med)
9

Overhead Press

Compound Push
Watch: Eric Leija Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Start with bell at chest/shoulder height
  • Press straight up, finish with bicep near your ear
  • Full lockout — straighten the arm completely at the top
  • Lower under control — don't drop it
  • Brace your core and squeeze your glutes to stabilize
  • Don't lean back — press the bell UP, not forward
Monday: 4×10/arm (Med-Heavy)  |  Wednesday: 3×12/arm (Med)  |  Friday: 4×8/arm (Heavy)
10

Bicep Curls + Shoulder Press

Accessory
Full Tutorial: Strongandfit.com

Form Cues

  • Start with bell at your side, arm fully extended
  • One continuous motion: curl to shoulder, then press to the sky
  • Don't pause between the curl and the press — make it fluid
  • Full lockout overhead, then reverse: lower to shoulder, lower to side
  • Keep your elbow close to your body during the curl
  • 12-13 reps per arm per set to reach 25 total
Monday: 3×12 (Med)  |  Wednesday: 3×15 (Light-Med)  |  Friday: 3×10 (Med-Heavy)
11

Tricep Extensions

Isolation
Full Tutorial: Strongandfit.com

Form Cues

  • Stand tall, hold the bell behind your head with both hands
  • Elbows stay tight to your head — don't let them flare out
  • Lower the bell behind your head for a deep stretch
  • Press up to full lockout overhead
  • Full range of motion: deep stretch at bottom, full extension at top
  • Keep your core braced — don't arch your back
Monday: 3×12 (Med)  |  Wednesday: 3×15 (Light-Med)  |  Friday: 3×10 (Med)
12

Side Extensions (Side Bends)

Core
Watch: Cavemantraining Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hold the bell in one hand at your side
  • Lean your upper torso to the opposite side of the weight
  • Feel the oblique stretch on the weighted side
  • Lean slowly and controlled — don't jerk
  • Return to upright, squeezing the oblique to pull yourself up
  • 12-13 per side per set
Monday: 3×12/side (Med)  |  Wednesday: 3×15/side (Med)  |  Friday: 3×10/side (Heavy)
13

Farmer's Walk

Carry
Watch: Kettlebell Farmer's Walk Demo

Form Cues

  • Hold the heaviest KBs you can manage, one in each hand at your sides
  • Walk with purpose — controlled, steady stride
  • Shoulders back and down — don't let them round forward
  • Core braced tight throughout the entire walk
  • Grip hard — squeeze the handles as tight as possible
  • Maintain upright posture — head up, chest proud, no leaning
Monday: 3×40s (Heaviest)  |  Wednesday: 2×45s (Heavy)  |  Friday: 3×40s (Heaviest)
