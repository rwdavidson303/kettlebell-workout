Standard Day

Monday Workout

Goal: Balanced baseline — solid effort across all 13 exercises
Pace: Steady — HR to 140-145 bpm between sets (~30 sec)
Time: 55-65 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Bell Notes Demo
1 Kettlebell Swings 5 x 15 Heavy Explosive hip snap every rep. Longer sets build sustained power output. Watch Demo
2 High Pulls 4 x 12 Med-Heavy Pull to chin height, elbows high and wide. Drive with hips, not arms. Watch Demo
3 Side (Golf) Swings 3 x 15 Medium Controlled rotation through core. 7-8 each direction per set. Watch Demo
4 Waist Halos 2 x 15 Light Active recovery — circles around the waist. Alternate direction each set. Watch Demo
5 Goblet Squats 4 x 12 Heavy Hold KB at chest, full depth squat. Drive through heels. Pause 1 sec at bottom. Watch Demo
6 Rows 4 x 12/arm Heavy Squeeze shoulder blade at top for 1 second. Control the lowering. Watch Demo
7 Push Outs 4 x 12 Med-Heavy Start at chest, press horizontally. Full arm extension. Controlled return. Form Cues
8 Head Halos 2 x 15 Light Active recovery — circles around the head. Alternate direction. Slow and controlled. Form Cues
9 Overhead Press 4 x 10/arm Med-Heavy Start at shoulder, press straight up to full lockout. 10 per arm per set. Watch Demo
10 Bicep Curls + Press 3 x 12 Medium One fluid motion: curl to shoulder, press to sky. 6 per arm per set. Form Cues
11 Tricep Extensions 3 x 12 Medium Elbows tight to head, full stretch behind head, full lockout at top. Form Cues
12 Side Extensions 3 x 12/side Medium 6 per side per set. Lean slowly, feel the oblique stretch. Watch Demo
13 Farmer's Walk 3 x 40s HEAVIEST Walk with purpose. Shoulders back and down, core braced. Grip hard. 40 seconds per walk. Watch Demo
Total: ~620 reps — 13 exercises, optimized volume

Cool-Down (5 Minutes)

  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes
  • Hip flexor stretch: 30 sec per side
  • Chest doorway stretch: 30 sec per side
  • Hamstring stretch (standing or seated): 30 sec per side
