Strength Day

Strength Workout

Goal: Power endurance — maximize force production with higher rep quality
Rest: 90-120 sec between sets
Tempo: 3-1-1 (3s down, 1s pause, 1s up)
Time: ~55 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes Demo
1 Bulgarian Split Squat 4 x 8/leg 25-30 lb 90s Rear foot elevated on bench. Drive through front heel. Controlled tempo. Form Cues
2 DB Romanian Deadlift 4 x 8 25-30 lb 90s Hinge at hips, soft knee bend. DBs slide along thighs. Feel hamstring stretch. Form Cues
3 DB Hip Thrust 3 x 10 25-30 lb 90s Shoulders on bench, DB on hips. Drive hips to full extension. Squeeze glutes 1 sec at top. Form Cues
4 Incline DB Press (30°) 4 x 8 25-30 lb 90s Bench at 30°. Lower to chest, 1-sec pause, press to lockout. Form Cues
5 One-Arm DB Row 4 x 8/arm 25-30 lb 90s Hand and knee on bench. Pull to hip, squeeze shoulder blade 1 sec. Form Cues
6 Chest-Supported Row 3 x 10 20-25 lb 90s Lie face-down on incline bench. Row both DBs, squeeze at top. Eliminates momentum. Form Cues
7 Standing OH Press 3 x 8 20-25 lb 90s Start at shoulders, press straight up. Core braced — no arching. Form Cues
8 Incline Curl 3 x 10 15-20 lb 75s Bench at 45°. Arms hang straight down. Curl without swinging. Form Cues
9 OH Tricep Extension 3 x 10 15-20 lb 75s One DB, both hands. Lower behind head slowly. Elbows point forward. Form Cues
10a SSLateral Raise 3 x 10 10-15 lb — Slight forward lean. Raise to shoulder height. Lead with elbows. Form Cues
10b SSBent-Over Rear Delt Fly 3 x 10 10-15 lb 60s Hinged at 45°. Squeeze shoulder blades at top. Form Cues
11 Farmer's Walk (40 sec) 3 x 1 25-30 lb 60s Walk with purpose. Shoulders back and down, core braced. 40 seconds. Form Cues
Total: ~330 reps — 38 working sets — Power endurance rep range
